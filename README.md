Features:
- Keyboard switches for play/pause, skip forward/back and stop
- Rotary volume control with integrated mute switch
- 0.91" OLED to display current song

Bill Of Materials:
- Seeed XIAO RP2040
- 4 through-hole 1N4148 Diodes
- 4 MX-Style switches
- 1 EC11 Rotary Encoder
- 1 0.91 inch OLED display
- 4 white blank DSA keycaps
- 4 M3x16mm screws

Files:
PCB (inc. Schematic) - KiCAD
CAD - OnShape
Firmware - QMK
